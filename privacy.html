Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. About Us

Normtech Ltd is the data controller for the personal data described in this policy. We are registered in England and Wales (Company No. 17105378). Our registered address is Castle House, Silver Street, Wethersfield, Essex, CM7 4BP.

2. What We Collect

The data we collect depends on which of our products you use. Across our Services, this may include:

  • Account information — name, email address, authentication identifiers
  • Payment information — processed by Stripe; we do not store card details
  • Content you provide — messages, files, images, or other inputs submitted to our products
  • Usage data — how you interact with our products, feature usage, and error logs
  • Device information — browser type, operating system, and IP address
  • Integration data — data from third-party services you connect (e.g. Google Calendar, Telegram, Canva)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our Services
  • Process payments and manage subscriptions
  • Respond to your requests and support enquiries
  • Send important notices about changes to our Services
  • Monitor and improve security, performance, and reliability

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under UK GDPR, we process your data on the following bases:

  • Contract — processing necessary to deliver the Services you have signed up for
  • Consent — where you authorise integrations with third-party services
  • Legitimate interest — usage analytics, security monitoring, and service improvement
  • Legal obligation — where required by law

5. AI Data Processing

Some of our products process data using third-party AI providers (currently Anthropic). When this applies:

  • Your data is transmitted to the AI provider's API for processing
  • Anthropic does not use API customer data to train or improve their models
  • Anthropic retains API data for a maximum of 30 days for safety purposes
  • Voice transcription, where offered, is processed locally and not sent to third parties

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with service providers necessary to operate our Services:

  • Anthropic (US) — AI processing
  • DigitalOcean (Frankfurt, Germany) — infrastructure hosting
  • Vercel (US) — web application hosting
  • Stripe (US) — payment processing
  • Cloudflare (US) — DNS and security

Where data is transferred outside the UK, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place.

7. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information, including encryption in transit and at rest. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active, plus 30 days after deletion to allow for recovery. Usage data is retained for up to 12 months. We delete data when it is no longer needed for the purposes described in this policy.

9. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Restrict or object to certain processing
  • Request portability of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time (where processing is based on consent)

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@normtech.co.uk. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO).

10. Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies. Where our products use web analytics, we use privacy-respecting, cookieless solutions. Individual products may set functional cookies necessary for the service to operate.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. For material changes, we will provide reasonable notice. Changes are effective when posted with an updated "Last updated" date.

12. Contact

For privacy-related enquiries, contact us at support@normtech.co.uk.
